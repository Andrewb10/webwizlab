Automated Sales Data Tracking and Reporting System

Overview:

Designed and implemented an advanced sales data tracking and reporting system to replace manual processes, improving efficiency, accuracy, and data accessibility for both individual agents and leadership teams. This system centralized key metrics, automated reporting, and enhanced visibility into sales performance, fostering a more informed and accountable work environment.

Responsibilities:

  • System Development: Built a comprehensive tracking system using SQL, enabling the precise monitoring of quotes, bookings, split commissions, and total sales for each sales agent. This system centralized data entry, eliminated manual tracking, and ensured accurate reporting.
  • Automated Reporting: Developed a robust reporting process using JavaScript that automatically generated and emailed detailed sales reports to admins and sales managers on a weekly and monthly basis. These reports included agent-specific performance metrics and comparative summaries for quick decision-making.
  • Real-Time Metrics Integration: Integrated a daily metrics update system into Slack, posting real-time sales data in designated channels. This ensured that teams remained informed about key performance indicators, and improved accuracy by replacing daily manual posting of data by automating this process.
  • Customizable Features: Incorporated scalable and customizable components, allowing for future adaptations to meet evolving business needs or add new data points for tracking.

Outcome:

The automated system significantly reduced time spent on manual tracking, saving hours each week for agents and managers. It improved data accuracy, delivered timely insights to leadership, and promoted a data-driven approach to decision-making. Detailed visibility into sales performance metrics improved accountability within the sales team, allowing them to focus on priorities and achieve results more effectively.

Scalable Event Confirmation Automation System

Overview:

Reimagined the customer confirmation workflow for upcoming events, transitioning from a manual, time-consuming process to a fully automated solution. This initiative significantly enhanced operational efficiency, improved communication consistency, and allowed teams to focus on strategic priorities.

Responsibilities:

  • System Development: Created an efficient automation solution using Zapier and custom JavaScript code, enabling bulk confirmation texts to be sent in minutes rather than hours.
  • Process Streamlining: Redesigned the workflow to allow staff to manage hundreds of customer confirmation messages in just a few simple steps, eliminating tedious manual texting.
  • Quality Control: Implemented seamless automation into daily operations, eliminating errors associated with manual texting and ensuring accurate, timely communication with customers.
  • Team Training: Guided staff through system adoption with hands-on training to ensure smooth implementation and maximize its impact on productivity.

Outcome:

The implementation reduced task time from 4 hours daily to just 3 minutes, saving over 20 hours of weekly staff time. In just 7 months of implementation, the new workflow process automated more than 14,800 messages and replies, ensuring consistent and timely customer communication, and freeing up valuable team resources while maintaining consistent customer communication quality.

Google Address Autocomplete Integration

Overview:

Overview:

Developed, documented, and implemented over 20 Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to ensure consistent, efficient task execution across the company. These SOPs addressed both existing workflows and new processes, ensuring documented processes were clear, easy to follow, and accessible for all employees.

Responsibilities:

  • Research and Development: Conducted in-depth research into best practices for each procedure, analyzing workflow requirements and pain points to create clear, streamlined SOPs that addressed both established and evolving needs.
  • Cross-Department Collaboration: Collaborated strategically with key department heads from HR, Marketing, Sales, Office Management, Field Staff Management, and Training Coordination to develop customized SOPs that respected each department's unique operational needs, comprehensively documenting workflows and standard protocols for consistent performance.
  • Implementation: Implemented seamless automation into daily operations, eliminating errors associated with manual texting and ensuring accurate, timely communication with customers.
  • Team Training: Created SOPs tailored to various learning styles, ensuring accessibility for all team members, and deployed them across multiple platforms to maximize reach and engagement. Including:
    • Step-by-Step Video Tutorials: Created engaging, carefully edited videos to walk users through procedures in real time.
    • Mockup Demonstrations: Enabled hands-on interaction with processes, allowing users to practice without any potential for error.
    • Voice-over Video Guides: Added auditory support to enhance understanding for auditory learners, aligning narration with the visual steps.
    • Trainual Publishing: Organized SOPs into structured sections, making steps easy to navigate and understand.
    • Google Sheets with Visual Aids: Provided a high-level overview through annotated examples, offering easy reference and shareability.

Outcome:

The comprehensive SOP suite improved team productivity and consistency in task execution, reduced training time for new hires, and ensured that all employees could confidently follow procedures regardless of learning preference. The new standards directly contributed to measurable improvements in task completion rates, reduced errors, and faster onboarding times, driving overall operational efficiency across the organization.
